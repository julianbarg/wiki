* Responses [@Oliver1991]
    * Acquisce
    * Compromise
    * Avoid

* Targets for responses

* Strategies

    * Challenge the enemy coalition--donate and lobby (NRA & coal)

* Temporal
    * Preemptive
    * Reactive
    * Delay
